About Me

WebDev path

Hey, I'm Joachim, a web developer driven by a thirst for learning and a passion for the arts.

With a diverse background spanning over 15 years in electronic music, art, and education, I've honed an obsessive eye for detail that guides me in every new challenge I tackle. From crafting immersive room installations for exhibitions to sharing my music on international labels, I've mastered combining the technical and creative work processes of collaborative and individual projects. Each project has instilled in me a sense of flexibility with new work settings, adaptability, and self-reliance, which I mastered in my previous mentoring and artist roles.


By completing a Full Stack Web Development Bootcamp I honed my skills in UI design, backend development, and database management. Its diverse technological combinations offer a perfect opportunity to blend my creative stance with detail-oriented planning to craft unique web applications.
