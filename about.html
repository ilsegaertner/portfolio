About Me

WebDev path

Hey, I’m Joachim — a web developer with roots in arts and sound, driven by curiosity and a love for learning.

Over the past 15 years, I’ve worked across electronic music, visual art, and education — crafting room installations, releasing music on international labels, and mentoring creative processes. This path taught me to move fluidly between concept and execution, collaboration and independence, structure and intuition. They’ve also shaped the way I approach development: with care, precision, and obsessive attention to detail — while leaving space for free, outside-the-box thinking.


After completing a Full Stack Web Development Bootcamp, I deepened my skills in UI design, frontend development, and database architecture. For me, web development is a space where creativity meets logic — a place to design thoughtful, responsive interfaces while planning with technical clarity. My work is grounded in visual sensitivity, analytical thinking, and a genuine interest in how people interact — both with interfaces and with each other.
