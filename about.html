About Me

WebDev path

Hey, I’m Joachim — a web developer with roots in arts and sound, driven by curiosity and a love for learning.

Over the past 15 years, I’ve worked across electronic music, visual art, and education — crafting room installations, releasing music on international labels, and mentoring creative processes. This path taught me to move from early concepts to final details — sometimes working solo, other times in close collaboration. I approach development with care and precision — while leaving space for unconventional ideas.

After completing a Full-Stack Web Development Bootcamp, I deepened my skills in UI design, frontend development, and database architecture. Today, I build modular and accessible interfaces using technologies like React, TypeScript, and Tailwind CSS. For me, web development lives at the intersection of logic and creativity — demanding technical clarity while leaving room for visual experimentation.

I’m especially drawn to intercultural and social contexts. Whether facilitating group dialogue or mentoring language learners, I’ve always been curious about how people connect and communicate.


I’m open to new collaborations — especially those that combine interface design, thoughtful engineering, and a meaningful purpose. If you’re working on something that resonates, feel free to reach out.
