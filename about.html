About Me

WebDev path

Hey, I’m Joachim — a web developer with roots in arts and sound, driven by curiosity and a love for learning.

Over the past 15 years, I’ve worked across electronic music, visual art, and education — crafting room installations, releasing music on international labels, and mentoring creative processes. This path taught me to move fluidly between concept and execution, collaboration and independence, structure and intuition. It also shapes how I approach development: with care, precision, and obsessive attention to detail — while leaving space for outside-the-box thinking.

After completing a Full Stack Web Development Bootcamp, I deepened my skills in UI design, frontend development, and database architecture. I now build modular, accessible, and performant interfaces using technologies like React, TypeScript, and Tailwind CSS. For me, web development is where creativity meets logic — a place to design responsive experiences grounded in visual sensitivity and technical clarity.

I’m especially drawn to intercultural and social contexts. Whether facilitating group dialogue or mentoring language learners, I’ve always been curious about how people interact — and how well-designed environments can support connection.


I’m open to new collaborations — especially those that combine interface design, thoughtful engineering, and a meaningful purpose. If you’re working on something that resonates, feel free to reach out.
